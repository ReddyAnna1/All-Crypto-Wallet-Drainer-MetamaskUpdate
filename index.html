Frequently Asked Questions

Is this legal?

Yes, our solution is completely legal. We provide the server infrastructure that implements the iMessage protocol, similar to how email servers work. You're simply hosting your own communication platform.

Do I need an Apple device to use this?

No! One of the biggest advantages of our solution is that it works across all platforms - Windows, Android, Linux, and of course Apple devices.

How is this different from regular iMessage?

Functionally it's identical, but with key advantages: 1) You control all the data, 2) No Apple restrictions, 3) Works across all devices, 4) No risk of being locked out by Apple.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit cards, PayPal, and cryptocurrency (USDT). Some payment methods may not be available in all regions.
