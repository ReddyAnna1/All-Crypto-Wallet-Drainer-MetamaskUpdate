Common Questions

Is this service legal?

Yes, our solution is completely legal. We provide server infrastructure that implements open protocols, similar to how email servers work. You maintain full control and ownership of your data.

Do I need an Apple ID?

No Apple ID is required. Our system creates independent accounts that work across all platforms without any dependency on Apple's services.

How is this different from iMessage?

It offers the same great messaging experience but with key advantages: 1) You control all data, 2) No Apple restrictions, 3) Works on all devices, 4) No risk of account lockouts.

What payment methods do you accept?
